By downloading, installing, and using the Mobile Application, you (also referred to as the "User") voluntarily give written consent to the processing of your Personal Information as outlined in this Policy.

Types of Data Collected

We may collect the following information about you, depending on how you use our products:

Information You Provide to Us:

  • Personal Data: Your name, email address, login, and password.
  • Profile Data: Any information you add to your profile.
  • Purchase Information: Data necessary to process payments for purchases or subscriptions. We do not store your financial data on our servers. All payments are processed through secure third-party services that comply with industry standards.

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our applications and website, and which features you use.

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Security: To protect our systems from fraud and unauthorized access.

We do not sell or exchange your personal information with third parties without your consent, except when it is necessary to provide our services or as required by law. The collection and use of your data by these services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, and restrict the processing of your personal data. You can contact us with any questions related to your rights.
